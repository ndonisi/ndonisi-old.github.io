Low Light Houseplants-Pothos

Also known by the name "Devil's Ivy", this plant is drought and low light tolerant. Pothos are so easy to propagate that you’ll be giving plants to your friends in no time. Simply cut off a piece of the stem anywhere from 6″ to 12″ long, strip off the lower leaves & place it in water.The lower the light, the less variegation &/or color your plant will have. Be careful not to overwater your Pothos, as it prefers 7-10 day apart waterings.
