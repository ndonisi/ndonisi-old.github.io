Feral Cat Coalition

Feral Cat Coalition of Oregon

Celebrating 25 years of helping 100,000+ cats and caregivers

FCCO is a spay/neuter program for cats.

Our services are no-cost for feral, stray, and barn cats (donations requested) and low-cost for pet cats, including vaccines. We serve Oregon and SW Washington.

We rely on your donations and volunteer time!
